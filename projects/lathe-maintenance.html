Centre Lathe Maintenance

Workshop Maintenance | Machine Care | Safety

Overview

This project involved carrying out routine maintenance on a centre lathe within the college workshop. The aim was to ensure the machine operated safely, accurately and reliably by inspecting critical components and performing cleaning and lubrication tasks.

The maintenance process included removing swarf and debris, checking machine guards and controls, lubricating sliding surfaces and ensuring the lead screw, carriage and chuck were operating correctly.

Routine maintenance is essential for extending machine life, maintaining precision and ensuring safe operation within a workshop environment.
